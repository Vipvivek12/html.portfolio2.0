About Vaishno Devi

Vaishno Devi is one of the most revered Hindu pilgrimage destinations. It is located in the Trikuta Mountains in Jammu and Kashmir, India.

How to Reach

You can reach Vaishno Devi by foot, helicopter, donkey/horse ride, or palki. The total distance to walk is approximately 13 kilometers.

Fooding and Lodging

There are various food and lodging options available along the way and near the temple. Ensure to book in advance during peak seasons.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Vaishno Devi is during the months of March to October. Avoid the monsoon season due to heavy rainfall.

Travel Tips

  • Be prepared for extremely cold weather; carry appropriate clothing.
  • Avoid using stairs during the journey as it can be very painful.
  • Stay hydrated and take breaks as needed.
